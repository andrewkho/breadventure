
doctor zeus zucchini bread

breads

3  eggs
1 cup oil
2 cup sugar
2 teaspoon vanilla
2 cup zucchini; shredded
8 1/2 oz crushed pineapple; drained
1 cup nuts; chopped
1/2 cup raisins
3 cup flour
1 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon nutmeg
2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon baking powder

beat the eggs, oil, sugar, and vanilla in a large bowl until thick.
stir in the remaining ingredients.  mix well. pour into 2 greased
loaf pans. bake at 350 for 1 hour. source: beverly hillbillies
cookbook

yield: 2 loaves
