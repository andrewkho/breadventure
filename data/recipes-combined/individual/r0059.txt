
baked apple doughnuts

breads, desserts, quick&easy

3 cup flour
3 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1 cup shortening
2 eggs -- beaten
1/2 cup milk
1 cup apples -- grated
1/2 cup chopped nuts

topping: 1/2 c butter -- melted 1/2 c sugar 2 tsps cinnamon

mix flour, baking powder, sugar, salt, and nutmeg. cut in shortening
until mixture looks like crumbs. add eggs, milk, apples, nuts and mix
thoroughly.

fill well greased tiny muffin pans 2/3 full.  bake in preheated
350-degree oven for 20-25 minutes.

in a plastic bag mix sugar and cinnamon.  dip doughnut tops in butter
and shake in bake to coat.

yield: 60 servings
