
charlo's zucchini bread

breads, vegetables, zucchini

3  eggs
1 cup oil
2 cup sugar (37 min left), (h)elp, more?
1 teaspoon salt
2 cup grated zucchini
2 teaspoon vanilla
3 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
3 cup flour
1 cup nuts (optional; i left them out)

mix wet ingredients, then add dry ingredients. pour into 2 loaf pans.
bake for one hour at 350 f.

this recipe doubles very easily. i could have made 4 loaves, but i
only had 3 loaf pans. i made 3 loaves, and some zucchini muffins.
