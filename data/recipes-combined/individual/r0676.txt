
crackling corn bread

breads, misc

1 1/2 cup corn-meal
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoon flour
2 teaspoon sugar
1  egg, well beaten
3 teaspoon baking powder
1 1/4 cup milk
1 1/2 cup cracklings

add flour, corn-meal, sugar, salt, baking powder, egg, and milk. add
cracklings.  beat thoroughly.  bake in well-oiled shallow pan in hot
oven (450 f) about 20 minutes, or drop by teaspoonfuls on a hot
well-oiled griddle.  fry until golden brown.  6 servings. virginia
cooper, new orleans, la.
