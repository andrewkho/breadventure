
breakfast muffins

breads, breakfast

1  egg
2 tablespoon sugar
1 cup sour cream
1 tablespoon shortening,melted
1 teaspoon prepared mustard
1 1/3 cup sifted flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 pkg (2 1/2 oz) carl buddig ham,
1 finely chopped
1/2 cup shredded wisconsin swiss
1  cheese

beat egg until light.add sugar,sour cream,shortening and mustard; stir
well.add dry ingredients;stir just until moistened;do not overbeat.
gently,stir in ham and cheese.fill greased muffin tins 3/4 full.bake

yield: 12 servings
