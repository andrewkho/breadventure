
feather-light muffins

breads

1/3 cup shortening
1/2 cup sugar
1  egg
1 1/2 cup cake flour
1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg, ground
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon, ground
1/2 cup butter, melted

in a mixing bowl, cream shortening, sugar and egg. combine dry
ingredients; add to creamed mixture alternately with milk. fill
greased muffin tins 2/3 full. bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes or until
golden. let cool for 3-4 minutes.
meanwhile, combine the 1/2 cup of sugar and 1 teasp cinnamon in a
cup. roll warm muffins in melted butter, then in sugar-cinnamon
mixture. serve warm. yield: 8-10

yield: 8 servings
