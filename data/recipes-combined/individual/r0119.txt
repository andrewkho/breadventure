
banana-pineapple bread

breads

1 cup chopped nuts
2 1/2 cup sugar
5 cup flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 1/2 cup vegetable oil
3  eggs
3  bananas, mashed
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 can crushed pineapple, drained

mix together dry ingredients.  mix together liquid ingredients;
combine. bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.

yield: 2 loaves
