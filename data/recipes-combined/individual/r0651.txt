
cornmeal sally lunn

breads

2 cup cornmeal
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/4 cup buttermilk
8 oz sour cream

combine cornmeal, baking soda and salt; add buttermilk and sour cream,
mixing well.  pour batter into a well-greased 9 inch pie plate. bake
at 350 degrees for 40 minutes or until lightly browned.

yield: 6 servings
