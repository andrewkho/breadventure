Portuguese Sweet Bread for the Mini Zo Bread Machine
    
    A southern New England favorite, this cake-like bread features hints of vanilla and lemon.
    
       152g evaporated milk 28g butter 1 large egg 1/4 teaspoon lemon oil 2 teaspoons vanilla extract 50g sugar 3/4 teaspoon salt 283g King Arthur Unbleached All-Purpose Flour 1 1/2 teaspoons SAF Gold instant yeast* *SAF Gold instant yeast is a strain of instant yeast specially developed for high-sugar and high-acid (sour) doughs. It's formulated to last longer, and to hold up better, than regular instant yeast in high-sugar or high-acid situations. 
    
     Directions   Place all ingredients into the pan of your machine, and program the machine for Basic White Bread. Press start.  When the bread is done, turn it out onto a rack to cool.
