
eva's zucchini bread

breads

3  eggs
1 cup vegetable oil
2 cup granulated sugar
2 cup shredded zucchini
2 teaspoon vanilla
3 cup flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon cinnamon
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup raisins (optional)

beat the eggs. add the oil and sugar, and mix well. preheat the oven
to 325 f. grease and flour 2 bread pans. mix all dry ingredients
together, and add sugar/egg/oil mixture to it. fold in the zucchini
and nuts (also raisins, if used) bake 1 1/2 hours.

yield: 8 servings
