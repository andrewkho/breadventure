
belgian loaf

breads

1 cup sugar
1 cup raisins
1 cup milk
1/4 lb shortening
1  egg
1 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon soda

in saucepan put first 4 ingredients and bring to a boil, cool. then
stir in 1 egg, then the dry ingredients, mix in 2nd. cup of flour.
bake at 350 for
45    min.

yield: 6 servings
