
crostini

breads, italian

1  (1-pound) loaf of italian bread
2  cloves of garlic, halved
2 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon dries rosemary, crushed

crostini

cut bread into 22 slices (1/2-inch thick); arrange in a single layer
on a baking sheet. rub garlic on one side of each piece of bread;
brush with olive and sprinkle with rosemary. discard garlic. bake
bread at 350 for 10 min. on each side until lightly brown. let cool
before serving. yield: 22 servings (about 68 calories per slice)
