
cheese drop biscuits

breads, cheese

2 cup flour
4 tablespoon butter
3 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
6 tablespoon cheese - ground

sift flour, salt, baking powder together. chop in butter. add enough
milk to make up soft. add cheese. drop on buttered baking tin. note:
no baking time or temperature is given. suggest moderate 400 f oven,
10 ~ 20 minutes.

yield: 1 servings
