
chocolate zucchini bread  ...( makes 2 loaves

breads

3  eggs
2 cup white sugar
1 cup oil
2  squares unsweetened
1  chocolate, melted
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cup grated zucchini
3 cup flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup nuts, ground

mix eggs,sugar and oil well!!  stir in melted chocolate and add
vanilla and stir in zucchini.  add dry ingredients slowly and mix
well. pour into 2 9x5x3 inch greased loaf pans..bake at 350 degrees
for 50 minutes or until they test done.  cool and turn out on cake
rack to finish cooling. from: sandee eveland
date:
14    aug 96

yield: 4 servings
