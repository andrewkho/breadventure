
corn dodgers

breads

2 cup whole ground self-rising cornmeal
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
2 tablespoon melted shortening
1 cup plus 2 tb, buttermilk

preheat oven to 450 degrees.  grease a large black iron skillet and
place in oven to heat.  measure cornmeal and soda into a bowl; add
melted shortening and buttermilk. beat thoroughly to blend.

dip hands into bowl of cool water and shape mixture into
golf-ball-size rounds; pull fingers across the top to shape into
disks, leaving ridges on top. place in hot skillet and bake about 25
minutes, or until dark brown and crusty.

yield: 12 servings
