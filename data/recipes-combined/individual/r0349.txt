
caramel-nut sticky biscuits

breakfast, snacks

      ----topping----
2/3 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 cup light corn syrup
1/4 cup (1/2 stick) margarine; melted
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 cup pecan halves
      ----biscuits----
2 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup quaker oats (quick or old
1  fashioned, uncooked)
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
3/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/3 cup (5-1/3 tb) margarine
1 cup buttermilk*

heat oven to 425~f.  for topping, combine first flour
igredients; mix well.  spread onto bottom of 9-inch square baking
pan.  sprinkle with pecans; set aside.
for biscuits, combine dry ingredients; mix in buttermilk, mixing
just until moistened.  knead gently on lightly floured surface 5 to 7
time; pat into 8-inch square. cut with knife into sixteen 2-inch
square biscuits; place over topping in pan.  bake 25 to 28 minutes or
until golden brown. let stand 3 minutes; invert onto large platter.
serve warm.

note: * sour milk may be substituted for buttermilk.  for 1 cup sour
milk, combine 1 tablespoon vinegar or lemon juice and enough milk to
make 1 cup, let stand 5 minutes.

yield: 16 servings
