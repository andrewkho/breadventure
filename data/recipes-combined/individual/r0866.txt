
easy graham peanut butter bread

breads, peanut butter

3 cup graham flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 cup sugar
1  egg
1 1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup peanut butter

mix the dry ingredients.  add the rest of the ingredients. mix well.
pour into a greased loaf pan. bake at 350 f. for 40 to 45 minutes.

yield: 18 servings
