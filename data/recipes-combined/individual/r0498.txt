
chocolate chunky mighty muffins

breads

1 1/2 cup flour
1 cup bran cereal flakes
1 cup rolled oats
1 1/2 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
6 tablespoon buttermilk powder
1 1/4 cup water
1 each egg
4 cup butter; or marg, melted this must
1 cup chocolate chunks
1 cup chopped nuts

mix dry and wet ingreds separately. combine--will be lumpy. stir in
chunks and nuts.

bake in lined muffin cups 25-30 mins, until toothpick test passes, and
mufins are browned.

yield: 1 servings
