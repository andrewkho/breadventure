
bisquick blueberry muffins

breads, fruits

1  egg
2 cup baking mix, (bisquick)
1/3 cup sugar
2/3 cup milk
2 tablespoon oil, vegetable
3/4 cup blueberries, fresh or frozen
1 (thawed and drained)

heat oven to 400 degrees. grease bottoms only of 12 medium muffin
cups or line with paper baking cups. beat eggs slightly in medium
bowl; stir in remaining ingredients except blueberries just until
moistened. fold blueberries into batter. divide batter evenly among
cups. bake 15-18 minutes or until golden brown.

yield: 12 servings
