
cannon balls

breads, cheese/eggs, main dish, meats, beef

1/4 single del's dinner roll recipe
8 oz raw ground beef
2 oz shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 each medium onion, chopped fine
1 single oil for frying

take a piece of dough and flatten it into a circle 2 to 3 inches in
dia. in the middle of the dough, place a small amount of meat, cheese
and onion. shape the dough into a ball and seal edges to enclose the
meat. repeat until all ingredients are used up. drop balls into hot
oil ( 3750f. ) and cook until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes.
variation: for breakfast, form dough into balls and deep fry to a
golden

yield: 8 servings
