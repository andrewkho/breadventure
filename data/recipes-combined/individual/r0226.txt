
bits of sunshine

appetizers, breads, cheese/eggs

1 cup margarine
8 oz sharp cheddar cheese; grated
1 cup sour cream
2 cup self-rising flour

melt margarine and remove from heat. add grated cheese.  stir and
cool 2 minutes.  add sour cream and mix well.  stir in flour.  fill
greased miniature muffin pans 2/3 full.  bake at 350 degrees for
20-25 minutes. yield: 48 miniature muffins.  (easy to make cheese
biscuits - very rich in taste!)
