
corn cake batter

breads

3 oz cornmeal
3 oz all purpose flour
2  eggs
8 oz buttermilk
1 tablespoon onion, diced fine
3 oz corn, fresh kernal
1 teaspoon salt

combine all ingredients in mixing bowl and whip until smooth. ladle
onto hot griddle to cook.

yield: 4 servings
