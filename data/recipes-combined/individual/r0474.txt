
chili cheese puff

breads, cheese/eggs, mexican, chili

5  eggs
1/4 cup all purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup cottage cheese
2 cup shredded monterey jack cheese (8oz)
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1 can (4oz) chopped green chilies, draine; d

in a large mixing bowl, beat eggs well.  stir in next five
ingredients. add green chilies.  pour into a greased 8 inch square
baking pan. bake at 350 f for 40-45 minutes or until a knife inserted
near the center comes out clean. serve immediately. makes 6-8
servings.

origin: mariane white, in reminisce march/april 1993 shared by: sharon
stevens

yield: 6 servings
