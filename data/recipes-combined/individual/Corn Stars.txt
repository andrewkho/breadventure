Corn Stars
    
    This cornbread with fresh corn will shine on your table, no matter the shape.
    
       170g yellow cornmeal 120g King Arthur Unbleached All-Purpose Flour or 113g White Whole Wheat Flour 25g sugar 1 1/2 teaspoons salt 2 teaspoons baking powder 3/4 teaspoon baking soda 3 large eggs 283g buttermilk 57g butter, melted 163g whole-kernel corn 
    
     Directions   Preheat the oven to 425Â°F. Grease six mini silicone star pans, or 12 paper-lined muffin cups.  Whisk together the cornmeal, flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and baking soda.  Whisk the eggs, buttermilk, melted butter, and corn together until evenly blended.  Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients.  Fill the prepared star pans or muffin cups 2/3 full.   Bake the stars or muffins for 20 to 25 minutes, until golden brown.  Remove from the oven, let cool in the pan for 5 to 10 minutes, then turn out of the pan onto a rack to cool completely.  Store the stars or muffins, well-wrapped, at room temperature for up to 2 days or freeze for up to a month.  
    
     Tips from our bakers  Leftover roast corn is the best in this recipe, just cut it off the cob.For a picnic or barbecue, split baked corn stars, butter, and heat briefly on a grill.
