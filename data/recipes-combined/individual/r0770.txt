
dark moist bran muffins

breads

2 cup whole wheat flour
1 1/2 cup bran flakes
2 tablespoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
2 cup buttermilk
1  egg
1/2 cup dark molasses
2 tablespoon melted butter or margarine

combine flour, bran, sugar, salt and soda and mix well. combine
buttermilk, egg, molasses and butter and add all at once to dry
ingredients. stir just enough to moisten flour mixture. fill greased
muffin cups 2/3 full and bake at 350f 20 to 25 minutes.

(c) 1992 the los angeles times

yield: 24 servings
