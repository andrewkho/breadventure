
celery croutes

appetizers, breads, side dish

4  slices bread
2 oz butter
1  celery seeds

remove crusts from bread; cut each slice into 3 fingers. melt butter
in small saucepan.  brush both sides of bread with butter. place on
greased cookie sheet, sprinkle lightly with celery seeds. bake in
moderate oven 5 to 10 minutes or until golden. turn croutes and brush
with butter during cooking. source: dinner party cookbook ch.

yield: 1 servings
