
bran cereal muffins

breads

1 cup whole bran cereal
1 cup milk
1  egg
1/4 cup oil
1/4 cup honey
1 1/4 cup whole wheat flour; unsifted
2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt

preheat oven to 400 f (hot).

grease muffin tins.

stir bran cereal and milk together in a bowl.  let stand a minute or
two. add egg, oil, and honey.  beat well. stir remaining ingredients
together until well mixed. add to liquid mixture and stir only until
mixed. put into muffin tins.

bake about 20 to 25 minutes or until lightly browned.

calories per muffin: about 135

source: food -- by u.s. department of agriculture typed for you by
karen mintzias

yield: 12 muffins
