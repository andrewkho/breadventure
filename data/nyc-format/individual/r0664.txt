
country pumpkin bread

breads

3/4 cup shortening
2 1/2 cup sugar
4  eggs, beaten
2 cup cooked pumpkin
2/3 cup water
3 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
2 teaspoon soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon allspice
1 cup black walnuts
2/3 cup raisins

preheat oven to 350. cream shortening, sugar, and eggs. stir in
pumpkin and water. mix together flour, baking powder, soda, salt, and
spices. stir dry ingredients into wet ingredients. fold in nuts and
raisins. spoon bater into two greased and floured 9x5" loaf pans and
bake for one hour, or until bread tests done with a toothpick. yield:
2 loaves

yield: 12 servings
