
baking powder biscuits ii

breads, biscuits

3 cup ready-mixed flour
7 tablespoon shortening
1 cup milk

sift flour, measure, and sift.  cut in shortening with 2 spatulas. add
milk.  turn onto lightly floured board. knead lightly and pat into
sheet 1/2 inch thick. cut with floured cutter. bake in hot oven (450
f) 12 minutes.  16 servings. the household searchlight - 1941
