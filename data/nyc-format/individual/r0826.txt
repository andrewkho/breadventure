
drew's famous onion dill bread

breads

      ----for 11/2 lb. loaf----
      ----add----
1 pkg yeast
3 1/3 cup flour
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1  egg; unbeaten, room temp
      ----mix together, warm and add----
1/4 cup water
3/4 cup cottage cheese
3/4 cup sour cream
3 tablespoon sugar
3 tablespoon minced dried onion
2 tablespoon whole dill seed
1 1/2 tablespoon butter

place dry ingredients and egg into inner pan. warm next set of
ingredients, add, select white bread, and push start. lightly brush
the top with a little melted butter at baking time if you wish.

yield: 1 servings
