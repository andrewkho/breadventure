
four-hour bread

breads

1 cup potatoes; mashed
1 quart potato water
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup sugar
1 each yeast cake
2 tablespoon lard
2 tablespoon salt

pour potato water, hot, over flour and sugar. add potatoes and cool.
add yeast. make sponge. beat 5 minutes, then let rise and add lard
and salt. stiffen up and knead at least 20 minuts. let rise again.
make into loaves or light cakes. let rise and bake

note:potato water is water left from boiling potatoes. source: estella
roberts, salt rock grange, marion county, oh

yield: 1 servings
