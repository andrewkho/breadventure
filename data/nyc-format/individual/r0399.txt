
cheddar & bacon bread

breads

2 1/2 teaspoon yeast
3 cup bread flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon sugar
1 1/3 tablespoon butter
1 cup milk
      ----add following 1st kneading----
2/3 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2/3 cup cooked, crumbled bacon

bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in
order. set "baking control" at 10 o'clock. select "white bread" and
push start. in hot & humid weather, use 1/8 c less water.

yield: 1 ounces
