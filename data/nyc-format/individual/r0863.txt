
easy doughnuts

breads, breakfast

3 cup flour
2 single eggs
2/3 cup sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup vegatable oil
1 cup milk
4 teaspoon baking powder
2 cup oil for deep fat frying

blend together sugar, milk, eggs and oil. add flour, salt, baking
powder mix all lightly. heat 2 cups oil in a 2 quart sauce pan to 365
f. drop dough by teaspoonful into hot oil. (if oil is to hot,
doughnuts will not get done inside) fry for 2 to 3 minutes or until
golden brown. turn frequently while cooking.drain on paper toweling
and roll in white sugar while still hot. hint: check the first couple
of doughnuts to see if they are o.k. and done.

yield: 12 servings
