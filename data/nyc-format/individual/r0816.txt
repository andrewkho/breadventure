
double bran citrus muffins

breads

1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour with wheat bran (; five roses)
1/2 cup oat bran
1/3 cup sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup buttermilk
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1  egg; lightly beaten
1  grated rind of 1/2 orange
1  grated rind of 1/2 lemon
1/2 cup dates or prunes; chopped
1 tablespoon sesame seeds

in a large bowl, combine flour, oat bran, sugar, baking powder,
baking soda and salt.  in a separate bowl, beat buttermilk, oil, egg
and citrus rinds. add to dry ingredients, mixing until just dampened
and still lumpy. lightly stir in dates and spoon into lightly greased
muffin cups. sprinkle with sesame seeds. bake at 375 f for 20 minutes.

yield: 10 muffins
