
cracklin' cornbread

breads

2 cup cornmeal
2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 teaspoon salt
2  eggs, beaten
2 cup buttermilk
2 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 cup cracklings

combine cornmeal, baking powder, soda and salt in a large bowl; add
eggs, buttermilk, oil and cracklings, stirring just until dry
ingredients are moistened.

place a well-greased 10-inch cast-iron skillet in a 450 degree oven
for 4 minutes or until hot. remove from oven; spoon batther into
skillet.

bake at 450 degrees for 25 minutes or until lightly browned.

yield: 8 servings
