
cheesy garlic bread 2

appetizers, breads, cheese/eggs, italian, garlic

1/2 cup butter or margarine, soft
4  cloves garlic, minced
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1  loaf french bread, halved
1  lengthwise (1 lb)
3 tablespoon grated parmesan cheese

in a small bowl, combine butter, garlic, and oregano; spread on cut
sides of bread.  sprinkle with parmesan cheese. place on an ungreased
baking sheet.  broil for 3 minutes or until golden brown. slice and
serve hot.

yield: 12 servings
