
chicken & dressing

breads, main dish

2 pkg cornbread mix
1  dozen raw eggs
1  chopped onion to taste
1  salt & pepper to taste
1  celery to taste
6 teaspoon poultry seasoning
3 teaspoon sage
2  chickens cooked & deboned

bake cornbread mix according to pk directions.  cool. crumble up
cornbread, add eggs, chopped onion & celery to taste, salt & pepper
to taste. add poultry seasoning & sage. add chicken & pour 1/2 of
chicken broth over cornbread mixture.  mix well.  bake at 350 degrees
for 3 hours.

~-

yield: 1 servings
