
baking powder drop biscuits+++fggt98b

biscuits, jewel

1 1/2 cup bake-it-all mix*
1/2 cup milk

*do not pack
make a well in the mix.  add milk and stir with a fork until all
of the mix is moistened.  drop dough by spoonfuls onto a cooky sheet.
for 6 biscuits, use 1 cup mix with about 1/3 cup milk.

yield: 9 servings
