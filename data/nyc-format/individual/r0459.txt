
cherry bread

breads

1/2 cup butter
3/4 cup sugar
2  eggs
1 teaspoon cherry soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cup sifted flour
1 teaspoon butttermilk
1 teaspoon vanilla
10 oz jar maraschino cherries,
1  chopped and drained

preheat oven to 350 degrees.  grease and flour a 9x5x3 inch pan.

in a large bowl, combine butter, sugar and eggs; beat with mixer until
creamy. add soda, salt, flour, and buttermilk. mix on low until
blended. fold in vanilla, cherries, and nuts. bake 60 minutes.

from ellen cleary

yield: 4 servings
