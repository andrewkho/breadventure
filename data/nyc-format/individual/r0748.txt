
crusty rye bread

breads

2 cup rye flour
1 tablespoon dry yeast
1/2 cup warm water
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 tablespoon cider vinegar
1/2 cup unbleached white flour

in a deep bowl, measure our the rye flour.  make a well in the centre.
dissolve the yeast in the water & add to the bowl. add salt, oil &
vinegar. stir vigorously. add the white flour. knead well till the
dough is pliable. form into a round bread.  use a brush to oil the
surface & set in a 9" round pan. allow to double in size. preheat
oven to 350f & bake for 1 hour 15 minutes.

yield: 1 loaf
