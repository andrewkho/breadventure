
bacon cheese bread

breads

1 cup tap water
3 cup flour
1/2 cup cheese (grated, sharp)
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 tablespoon sugar
1 1/2 tablespoon butter
1 1/2 teaspoon dry yeast
3 tablespoon bacon bits

add the bacon bits when beeping sounds during kneading process. place
ingredients into pan according to manufacturer's directions. press
start.

yield: 1 servings
