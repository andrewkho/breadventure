
cheese biscuits

breads

1/4 lb cheddar cheese, grated
1/4 lb butter or oleo
1 1/4 cup flour
1  pinch of salt

grate cheese and cream with butter.  add flour, salt and sugar;
chill. make into tiny biscuits, put 1/2 pecan on top of each. bake at
400 f. for 10 minutes. reduce heat to 350 for 10 minutes. courtesy
telephone pioneers billspalding *p crbr 38 a

yield: 1 servings
