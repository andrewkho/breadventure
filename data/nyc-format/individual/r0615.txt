
corn bread sticks

breads, misc

1 1/2 cup corn-meal
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 tablespoon sugar
1/2 cup flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 cup sour milk
2  eggs, well beaten
3 tablespoon melted shortening

sift flour, measure, and sift with salt, baking soda, baking powder,
and sugar.  combine eggs, milk, and shortening. add dry ingredients.
beat thoroughly. fill well-oiled corn-stick pans 2/3 full. bake in
hot oven (425 f) 20-25 minutes. 12 servings. the household
