
baking powder biscuits+++fggt98b

biscuits, jewel

3 cup bake-it-all mix*
3/4 cup milk

*do not pack
make a well in the mix.  add milk and stir with a fork, about 25
strokes.  turn onto a sheet of waxed paper. knead well 6 times. roll
out or pat to 1/2 inch thickness on a lightly floured pastry cloth or
board. cut into 2-inch rounds with floured cutter. place on a cooky
inches in diameter note:  for 6 biscuits, use 1 cup mix with 1/4 cup
milk.

yield: 18 servings
