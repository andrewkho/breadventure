Texas Toast
    
    Thick-sliced bread, anointed with sweet butter and garlic olive oil, grilled or griddled and served hot and crisp â€” now THAT'S Texas Toast!
    
       1 loaf English Muffin Toasting Bread,  cut into 8 to 10 slices 35g garlic oil 43g melted butter 
    
     Directions   Preheat a griddle to medium heat.   Cut the bread into 3/4" to 1" thick slices.  Combine the garlic oil and melted butter, and spread it on both sides of each piece of bread.  Fry the bread until golden brown on each side.  Serve hot.  Yield: 8 to 10 servings.  
    
     Tips from our bakers  This toast is traditionally prepared on an outdoor barbecue grill. If you've got your grill going, give it a try.
