Sandwich Bread for the Mini Zo Bread Machine
    
    King Arthur has a number of devoted bread-machine bakers, and this is everyone's favorite sandwich loaf. High-rising, close-grained, and with a hint of sweetness, it slices without crumbling and keeps well on the counter. This version is perfect for Zojirushi's 1-pound bread machine.
    
       1 teaspoon instant yeast 1 tablespoon + 2 teaspoons sugar 1 1/4 teaspoons salt 57g lukewarm milk 25g vegetable oil or 1 ounce butter 152g lukewarm water 283g King Arthur Unbleached All-Purpose Flour 
    
     Directions   Place all ingredients into the pan of your machine, and program the machine for Basic White Bread. Press start.  Remove the bread when it's done, and turn it out of the pan onto a rack to cool.
