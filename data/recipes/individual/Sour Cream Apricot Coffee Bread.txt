Sour Cream Apricot Coffee Bread
    
    This bright-gold, just-sweet-enough loaf is studded with apricots, and scented with citrus. The sour cream helps keep it moist.
    
       113g sour cream 2 large eggs 170g orange juice 1/8 teaspoon Fiori di Sicilia, optional, but delicious 418g King Arthur Unbleached All-Purpose Flour or 397g Pastry Flour Blend 50g sugar 11g SAF Red instant yeast or 7g SAF Gold instant yeast 1 1/2 teaspoons salt 46g potato flour or instant potato flakes 96g shredded or diced dried apricots 
    
     Directions   Place all of the ingredients into the pan of your bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.  Program the machine for the basic setting, light crust, and press Start.   Check the dough's consistency about 10 minutes before the end of the final kneading cycle, adding additional water or flour to form a soft, smooth ball of dough.  Allow the machine to complete its cycle.   Store the bread, well-wrapped, at room temperature for up to 5 days or freeze for up to 3 months.
